Empowering Stories, Igniting Connections

ISEM ILOCANDIA (smile ilocandia magazine) is a new concept publication, that is designed to touch the hearts and minds of the Filipinos, both globally and locally, especially those with heritage from Northern Luzon: ILOCOS REGION - CAGAYAN VALLEY - CORDILLERA REGION, if only to remind them that behind the natural beauty and rich cultural heritage of NORTHERN LUZON, are our hardworking kababayans' unwavering pursuit for a better life and global competitiveness, which we believe would be enough reason to bring SMILES to everyone.

Empowering Stories, Igniting Connections

Located in Dili, Bauang, La Union, DAScreatives Publishing is the home of ISEM Ilocandia – the lifestyle and leisure magazine of Northern Luzon that will make you smile.

We empower stories, ignite connections, and celebrate the essence of Northern Luzon through publications and events. With a passion for creativity and a commitment to excellence, we deliver exceptional publishing and event services that leave a lasting impact.

Our Mission

To craft compelling content, create unforgettable experiences, and build lasting relationships through our publications and events, while promoting the best of llocandia.

Our Vision

To be the premier publishing and events company in Northern Luzon, known for showcasing the region's rich culture, vibrant lifestyle, and inspiring stories..
